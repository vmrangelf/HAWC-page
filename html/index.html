Outriggers Interactive Layout

This main objective of this web-page is to keep informed about the outriggers distribution around HAWC at sierra negra.

 

 

 

How to use this web-page:

  • Select an option in the left banner.
  • Click on an outrigger, junction or Y-connector to display its relevant information (like coordinates, distance, etc.)
  • Click on Nomenclature to know about the symbol convention used in the layouts.

 

 

 

Refereces

  • The outriggers coordinates were taken from: internal HAWC page

  • The layout templates, junctions coordinates and distances were provided by Universidad Politécnica de Pachuca's and site-INAOE's cabling group.
